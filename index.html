Future Talent Programme

The Future-Talent-Programme (FTP) is a three-month program where students are taught C++ and Python through practical projects using microcontrollers like Arduino and NodeMCU. As part of the program, students are also guided to take part in national and global hackathons. They work on real-world problems, gaining experience and improving their skills by building solutions step by step. FTP is all about helping students learn by doing and preparing them for challenges in the tech world.
